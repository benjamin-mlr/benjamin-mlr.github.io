Benjamin Muller

PhD student in Natural Language Processing

INRIA

Sorbonne Université

I am a third-year PhD student at Sorbonne Université and INRIA Paris in the Almanach research team. My research focuses on understanding the behavior of large scale language models and applying them efficiently in the multilingual context. I have interned at Apple AI/ML and Amazon Alexa AI. I am also the main instructor for the Machine Learning for NLP course at ENSAE Paris and mentor for the Fatima Fellowship.
